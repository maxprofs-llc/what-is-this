The quintet performs twice a year at the School of Music. They also perform in the larger Tucson region, in competitions, and internationally.

Who is Fred Fox?

The Fred Fox Graduate Wind Quintet, formerly known as the Arizona Graduate Winds, was renamed in August 2012 in honor of a generous donation on behalf of renowned horn player Fred Fox and the UA School of Music.

Learn more about Fred Fox

Alumni

Former members of the Fred Fox Graduate Wind Quintet have successfully gone on to pursue careers within their field after graduating from the University of Arizona. They hold positions as teachers, doctorate students, and musicians in professional orchestras.
